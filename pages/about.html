About Shinelix

Crafting excellence since 1975

Our Story

For over five decades, Shinelix has been synonymous with exceptional craftsmanship and timeless elegance. Founded in 1975 by master jeweler Maria Shinelix, our brand has been dedicated to creating pieces that tell stories, celebrate moments, and become treasured heirlooms.

Every piece in our collection is meticulously handcrafted by skilled artisans who share our passion for perfection. We source only the finest materials - from ethically mined diamonds to lustrous pearls and precious metals - ensuring that each creation meets our exacting standards.

Our Mission

To create jewelry that transcends trends and becomes part of your personal story. We believe that true luxury lies not just in the materials we use, but in the emotions our pieces evoke and the memories they help create.
